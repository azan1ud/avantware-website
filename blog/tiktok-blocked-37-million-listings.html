Why TikTok Blocked 37 Million Listings — And How to Protect Yours

TikTok Shop removed over 37 million product listings in 2024 — and enforcement has only intensified since. In the first half of 2025 alone, TikTok took down over 2 million products for IP violations and removed 143 million videos globally for counterfeit-related breaches. Here's what sellers need to know heading into 2026.

TikTok Shop's enforcement wave isn't slowing down

TikTok Shop has grown explosively since its US launch, with millions of sellers rushing to list products. But with that growth came a massive crackdown. TikTok's internal compliance systems flagged and removed over 37 million listings in 2024 — and the pace has accelerated sharply since.

In just the first half of 2025, TikTok proactively took down over 2 million products for intellectual property violations, reactively removed 3.8 million items for IP and counterfeit breaches, and pulled 143 million videos globally for marketing counterfeit goods. The platform has explicitly shifted from a growth-first strategy to a compliance-first one.

The removals hit across every category: health claims in product descriptions, restricted keywords in titles, unverified safety certifications, and intellectual property issues. Many sellers didn't even know they were violating a policy until their listing — or their entire store — disappeared.

Why sellers keep getting blindsided

The rules are long and constantly changing

TikTok Shop's seller policies run into the hundreds of pages. They cover everything from prohibited claims ("cures", "treats", "clinically proven") to image requirements, category-specific restrictions, and regional regulatory rules. These policies update frequently — sometimes weekly — and sellers rarely get notified of changes.

One bad keyword can take down a listing

A single word in your product title or description can trigger an automated removal. Words like FDA, organic (without certification), anti-inflammatory, or even miracle are enough to flag a listing. The algorithms don't consider context — they pattern-match and remove.

Strikes add up fast

TikTok Shop operates on a points-based violation system. Each removed listing adds points to your account. Accumulate enough and your entire store gets suspended — not just the offending listing. Enforcement actions range from listing removal to permanent account suspension and fund withholding. Sellers have reported losing access to their entire shop over a handful of infractions they didn't realise they were committing.

New 2026 rules target content quality too

Starting in January 2026, TikTok Shop now enforces interaction-based limits on product-linked videos. Creators posting 5 or more "non-interactive" TikTok Shop videos within 7 days can trigger restrictions on their account. This means even your promotional content strategy can land you in violation territory — not just your listing text.

One seller had 200+ active listings taken down overnight after a policy update changed the rules around supplement descriptions. No prior warning, no grace period.

What gets flagged most often

  • Health and medical claims: Any language suggesting a product treats, cures, or prevents a condition — even if you're quoting the manufacturer.
  • Restricted keywords: Words that imply regulatory approval you don't have (FDA-approved, clinically tested, dermatologist-recommended without proof).
  • Missing certifications: Products in regulated categories (children's toys, electronics, cosmetics) that lack required safety documentation.
  • IP violations: Using brand names, logos, or trademarked terms — even in comparison language like "similar to [Brand]".
  • Misleading images: Before/after photos, results claims in images, or images that don't match the actual product.

How TikTok Shop Guard protects your listings

TikTok Shop Guard is a Chrome extension that scans your product listings before you publish them. It checks your titles, descriptions, and product details against TikTok Shop's current policy database and flags issues while you're still in the editor.

How it works

  • Install the Chrome extension: Add TikTok Shop Guard from the Chrome Web Store. It integrates directly into the TikTok Shop seller dashboard.
  • Write your listing as normal: Create or edit your product in TikTok Shop's listing editor.
  • Scan before publishing: Click scan and TikTok Shop Guard analyses your title, description, bullet points, and category for policy violations.
  • Fix flagged issues: Each flag shows the specific violation, the policy it relates to, and a suggestion for how to fix it.
  • Publish with confidence: Once your listing is clean, publish knowing it won't get pulled down an hour later.

Prevention beats recovery

Getting a listing removed is more than an inconvenience. It costs you sales velocity, review momentum, and algorithmic ranking — all things that are hard to rebuild on TikTok's platform. A suspended store is even worse: TikTok can permanently withhold funds in your seller account.

With enforcement volume up dramatically in 2025 and new content-quality rules in 2026, the window for "figure it out as you go" is closing. The most successful TikTok Shop sellers aren't the ones who never make mistakes. They're the ones who catch mistakes before TikTok does.
