Forex Calculator

Position Sizing & Risk in One Click. Instant position sizing, pip value, and risk calculations right in your browser. Supports all major and exotic pairs.

Get Started Back to Products
Key Features

Everything you need for forex risk management

Position Sizing

Calculate the optimal lot size based on your account size and risk percentage to stay within your risk tolerance on every trade.

Pip Value Calculator

Get instant pip values for any currency pair so you know exactly how much each pip movement is worth in your account currency.

Risk/Reward Ratio

Visualize your risk-to-reward ratio before entering any trade, helping you make informed decisions and maintain consistency.

Multi-Pair Support

Covers majors, minors, and exotic currency pairs so you can calculate position sizes and pip values for any instrument you trade.

Quick Access

Always one click away in your browser toolbar. No need to switch tabs or open separate apps when you need to size a position fast.

How It Works

Three steps to precise position sizing

Open the Extension

Click the Forex Calculator icon in your browser toolbar to launch the calculator instantly, no matter what page you're on.

Enter Parameters

Set your account size, risk percentage, and stop loss distance. Select your currency pair and the calculator does the rest.

Get Results

Instantly see your recommended lot size, pip value, and full risk breakdown so you can enter your trade with confidence.
