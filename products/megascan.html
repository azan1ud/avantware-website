Key Features

Everything you need for MegaETH token screening

Token Scanner

Real-time scanning of new token launches on the MegaETH network. Catch new listings the moment they go live.

Liquidity Analysis

Track liquidity pools across MegaETH DEXs. Monitor pool depth, volume, and liquidity changes in real time.

Wallet Tracking

Monitor whale wallets and smart money movements. See what top traders are buying and selling on MegaETH.

Smart Contract Audit

Basic contract safety scores for every token. Identify potential risks like honeypots, hidden mints, and rug pulls.

Price Alerts

Custom notifications for price movements, volume spikes, and liquidity events. Never miss a trading opportunity.

Portfolio Dashboard

Track your MegaETH holdings in one place. View PnL, allocation breakdowns, and historical performance at a glance.

How It Works

Three steps to smarter token screening

Connect to MegaETH

Plug into the MegaETH chain for live data. MegaScan connects directly to the network for real-time token and transaction feeds.

Set Your Filters

Define token age, liquidity thresholds, holder requirements, and more. Build custom screeners tailored to your trading strategy.

Monitor & Act

Get real-time alerts when tokens match your criteria. Act fast with one-click links to charts, contracts, and DEX trading pages.
